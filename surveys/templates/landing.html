Welcome!

These two surveys gather data and anectdotes from both software users and software developers, on their habits, thoughts, and feelings relating to information security.

The purpose is to contribute to a growing understanding of users and developers among security professionals.

The data will be presented at the Diana Initiative 2019, and I'll make it available at redandblack.io sometime after that. You can contact me on Twitter if you'd like to be told when the information is available.

Please don't fill either survey out more than once. I am relying on a honour system instead of collecting identifying information to ensure data integrity, so that you can be anonymous and feel comfortable to be completely honest.

Thanks for your time!
